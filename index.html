How to apply a discount code?

Here are the steps for you to use your discount code!

Step 1: Log In

  1. Open your web browser and go to Airvana.co.
  2. Click on "Log in."
  3. Enter your email address and password, or use your Google account if you registered with it.
  4. Click "Sign In" to access your account dashboard.

Step 2: Upgrade Your Membership

  1. Click on "Membership."
  2. Confirm your current membership is the Starter plan (free).
  3. Click on "Upgrade" to view available membership options.
  4. Select the membership that best suits your needs. Prices will be displayed.
  5. Click on "Continue Nationwide."

Step 3: Apply the Discount Code

  1. Ensure you have a payment method added under the “Billing” tab in your profile.
  2. Enter your discount code in the text box on the billing page.
  3. Click "Apply" to activate the discount.
  4. Verify the discount has been applied by checking the Billing Summary for a reduced total. If the total doesn’t change, try re-applying the code or refreshing the page.

Step 4: Complete the Purchase

  1. Click "Pay Now."
  2. Once the transaction is completed, you will see a confirmation page.

Congratulations!

Now you have full access to all Investor Hub features on Airvana.
